VISION

The Vision of Bingo Technology is to become the leading high tech renewable energy saving (Self Charging units) innovation globally.

Our vision is to provide our customers exceptional values by ensuring excellence in our products and services.

Bingo Technology's mission is to innovate quality and cost-effective renewable energy products and services, we will also improve carbon emissions through our new technology and hardware devices.

Meet the socio-economic needs of high temperature zones by creating renewable energy that are sustainable and that are environmentally friendly.

MISSION

THE PROBLEM

  • High carbon and its impact on our environment.
  • Issue of energy conservation and Independent.
  • High cost of energy produced and its environmental impact.

THE SOLUTION

The production of self charging powered (Ceiling Fans, charging of Cell Phones, Laptops and other handheld devices) and reducing reliance on the grid.

This will result in significant reduction of energy bills. The innovation will bring a clean energy project benefiting the community and heading towards sustainable targets.
